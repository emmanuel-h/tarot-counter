# Privacy Policy for Tarot Counter

**Effective Date:** April 2026

## 1. Overview

Tarot Counter is a simple scoring application for the tarot card game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## 2. What Data We Collect

**We collect no personal data.** Period.

Specifically, Tarot Counter:
- Does not require an account or login
- Does not collect any personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Does not track your location, device ID, or advertising ID
- Does not integrate with analytics services
- Does not make network requests to transmit any data

## 3. How Data Is Stored

All game data (player names, scores, game history) is stored **locally on your device only**. This data:
- Is never transmitted over the internet
- Is never shared with third parties
- Is never sold or used for marketing
- Remains entirely under your control

You can view and delete all stored data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data through your device settings.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Tarot Counter does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app contains no embedded trackers, SDKs, or external APIs that collect information about you or your usage.

## 5. Children's Privacy

Tarot Counter is suitable for users of all ages. Since we collect no data whatsoever, we do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 (or the digital age of consent in your jurisdiction).

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon publication. Your continued use of Tarot Counter constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

## 7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact us through the GitHub repository:
https://github.com/emmanuel-h/tarot-counter

---

**Summary:** Tarot Counter stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or analyze any personal information. Your privacy is completely protected.
